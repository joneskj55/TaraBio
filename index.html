Entrepreneurial Journey


Tara, a health technology expert with over 14-years of cross-functional healthcare experience at Johnson & Johnson, co-founded DIG Labs in 2019 when she decided to dedicate the next chapter of her life to helping pets better communicate with their parents. She holds a B.S. in Materials Science & Biomedical Engineering from Cornell University and an MBA from NYU Stern. Tara is also a registered patent agent. She holds 7 patents + 2 pending, spanning biomedical nanotechnology/ oncology-based imaging to consumer and pet technologies, and numerous peer-reviewed publications. Tara has been featured in Business Insider, Thrive Global, and Crunchbase. Tara lives in New Jersey with her husband, son, and two fur babies, Sam and Juney.

Dig Labs


DIG Labs is re-imagining pet health tech with real-time, in-your-pocket tools and personalized solutions. An early-stage, female-founded, NY-based venture-backed startup, DIG Labs is founded by former CPG R&D, brand management execs and pet lovers whose vision is to translate the benefits of personalized human healthcare to our pets. DIG Labs is actively launching its proprietary health app, powered by patent-pending computer vision & machine learning.
